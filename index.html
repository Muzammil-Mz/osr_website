Sathi Babu - Art Director

Founder & Director OSR Arts

With over 25 years of experience in the Telugu film industry, Our Founder has established OSR Arts as a premier name in film production support and event management. His visionary approach and meticulous attention to detail have made him the go-to expert for directors and producers seeking exceptional set designs and props.

From Tollywood movie sets to high-profile private events, Sathish's artistic vision has transformed countless productions across the Telugu cinema landscape. His work spans historical dramas, mythological epics, contemporary films, and elaborate event installations.
